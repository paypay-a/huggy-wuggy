Description

A mod of Friday Night Funkin Boyfriend rhythm-battle against Kissy Missy from Poppy Playtime.

Songs:

  • Kissy
  • Lovely
  • Playtime (freeplay)

Mod Credits:

  • Hyper-E : Sprites, playtime cover, artwork
  • WaddleGTB: music and charting

Original Huggy Wuggy Mod Credits:

If you had fun and would like to keep up-to-update on the mod, make sure to support mod creators on their social media by subscribing, following, liking, etc.

  • KJ Elliott: Art, Music and some Programming
